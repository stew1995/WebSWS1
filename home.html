What we do at Kidz Code!

  • - Here at Kidz Code, we recognize the shortage of programmers and the importance to society. We also understand that children of today will fill vital roles in tech such as these. That is why here at Kidz Code, we believe all children deserve the oppurtunity to learn how to code.
  • - We are not going to pretend that coding is easy, but it sure is rewarding. There is nothing more rewarding than seeing something you have built yourself.
  • - We teach children ages 5-14, how to code using several different languages. Before you know it, you or your child will be building; games, websites and simple applications.
